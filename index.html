    FNDR DFNDR

    This group project was developed in order to assist people with small garages. The goal was to provide the user with a way to park in their garage safely without bumping into the wall. It used an ultrasonic sensor to detect a vehicles distance from the wall and an led strip that would turn either green or red depending on how close they were to the wall.
